History

  • It all started in September 2025. On SJTU's cosmopolitan Qibao campus, three passionate students from different countries—Owen, Anqi, and Elson—envisioned more than just a club. They dreamed of a dynamic space where every coding enthusiast, from curious beginners to aspiring developers, could connect, collaborate, and grow together. This vision sparked the founding of the SJTU Pre-U Programming Club, transforming a simple idea into a thriving community that celebrates shared knowledge and creativity.

Vision

  • Create the most unforgettable, interactive, and wonderful collaborative experience! We aim to be the heart of tech innovation on the Qibao campus, a community that leaves a lasting mark on every member's Pre-U journey. Our vision is to build a legacy of projects, friendships, and skills that extend far beyond the classroom.

Mission

  • Through cooperation, creativity, and hands-on building, we craft engaging projects, tackle real-world problems, and share our passion for technology with the world. We empower every member to contribute, learn, and lead, fostering an inclusive environment where great ideas are born and brought to life.

Why Join the SJTU Pre-U Programming Club?

  • More Than Code:​ We combine academic excellence with practical application, creating a launchpad for personal and professional growth.
  • Inclusive Community:​ All skill levels are welcome! Our core belief is that everyone has something to teach and something to learn.
  • Project-Driven Learning:​ Move beyond theory. We build web projects that matter, developing portfolios and teamwork skills.
  • Qibao Campus Spirit:​ We are an integral part of the SJTU Pre-U Qibao community, designed to enhance your campus life with meaningful connections and exciting challenges.
