关于我们

Who We Are

We, three passionate students from three different countries — Owen, Anqi, and Elson — envisioned more than just a club.

We dreamed of a dynamic space where every coding enthusiast, from curious beginners to aspiring developers, could connect, collaborate, and grow together.

This vision sparked the founding of the SJTU Pre-U Programming Club, transforming a simple idea into a thriving community that celebrates shared knowledge and creativity.

我们的愿景

Our Vision

To create the most unforgettable, interactive, and wonderful collaborative experience! We aim to be the heart of tech innovation on the Qibao campus, a community that leaves a lasting mark on every member's Pre-U journey. Our vision is to build a legacy of projects, friendships, and skills that extend far beyond the classroom.

我们的使命

Our Mission

Through cooperation, creativity, and hands-on building, we craft engaging projects, tackle real-world problems, and share our passion for technology with the world. We empower every member to contribute, learn, and lead, fostering an inclusive environment where great ideas are born and brought to life.
